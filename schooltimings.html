Ramadan Timings

Please show this to your parents and review the timings.

Official Ramadan Lesson Timings

  • Monday – Thursday: 8:10 AM – 1:15 PM
  • Friday (Unchanged): 7:50 AM – 12:00 PM

Foundation Stage

  • Gate 2 opens at 7:45 AM – all FS children must be supervised by an adult.
  • All other gates open from 8:10 AM – 8:30 AM. Children will be marked late after this time.
  • Lessons start at 8:30 AM.
  • Dismissal: FS1 & FS2 from 1:00 PM – 1:15 PM (11:15 AM on Friday).
  • No Wrap Around Care during Ramadan.

Primary School

  • Gate 2 opens at 7:45 AM for working families. Children (Year 1+) can be dropped off at the gate and will be supervised by the leadership team on the Astro Turf.
  • All other gates open from 8:10 AM – 8:30 AM. Children will be marked late after this time.
  • Lessons start at 8:30 AM.
  • Dismissal: Year 1 to Year 6 from 1:00 PM – 1:15 PM (11:15 AM on Friday).

Secondary School

  • Gate 2 opens at 7:45 AM for early arrivals.
  • Gate 5 opens at 8:10 AM.
  • Registration: 8:10 AM – 8:30 AM. Children will be marked late after this time.
